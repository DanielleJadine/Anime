Featured Anime

Attack On Titan

"Attack on Titan," also known as "Shingeki no Kyojin" in Japanese, is a popular anime series based on the manga of the same name written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to the existence of giant humanoid creatures known as Titans, who devour humans seemingly without reason.

Naruto

Naruto's journey begins with his childhood as an orphan ostracized by his fellow villagers due to the presence of the Nine-Tailed Fox demon sealed within him. Despite facing adversity and loneliness, Naruto possesses an indomitable spirit and determination to prove his worth and earn the respect of his peers. He enters the Ninja Academy and forms bonds with classmates, including Sakura Haruno and Sasuke Uchiha, who become integral to his journey.

Pokemon

The Pokémon world is inhabited by humans and Pokémon who coexist in harmony. Trainers travel through various regions, each with its own unique Pokémon species, landscapes, and challenges. Notable regions in the Pokémon world include Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Kalos, Alola, and Galar.

Dragon BallsZ

The story of "Dragon Ball Z" continues the adventures of Goku, a powerful martial artist and defender of Earth, as he faces even greater challenges and adversaries. The series begins with Goku living a peaceful life with his family and friends after defeating the evil King Piccolo in the original "Dragon Ball" series. However, his tranquility is short-lived as a new threat emerges in the form of Raditz, Goku's long-lost brother and a member of the Saiyan warrior race
